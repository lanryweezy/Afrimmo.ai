## üìà On-Page SEO for Real Estate

### Property Listing Pages
- **Title Format**: "[Property Type] for Sale/Rent in [Location] | $[Price] | Afrimmo AI"
- **Meta Description**: "Find your dream [property type] in [location] for $[price]. View photos, details, and contact agent. Powered by Afrimmo AI."
- **Header Structure**: H1 for property address, H2 for features, H3 for neighborhood info
- **Image Alt Text**: "Modern 3-bedroom apartment in Victoria Island, Lagos with sea view"
- **Internal Links**: Link to similar properties, neighborhood guides, and agent profiles

### Agent Profile Pages
- **Title Format**: "[Agent Name] - Top Real Estate Agent in [Location] | Afrimmo AI"
- **Meta Description**: "Work with [Agent Name], top-rated real estate agent in [location]. Expert in [specialty]. Contact via Afrimmo AI."
- **Schema Markup**: RealEstateAgent schema with ratings, specialties, and areas served

### Neighborhood Guides
- **Title Format**: "[Neighborhood Name] Living Guide | Cost, Schools, Transport | Afrimmo AI"
- **Content**: Local amenities, cost of living, schools, transport, crime stats
- **Internal Links**: Link to properties in the area, agent profiles, and market reports
